About the Project

Back in 2021, I wanted to see how far I could push the visual boundaries of mobile design (small screen) by blending immersive art with functional interfaces. This section is my personal "visual lab" from my freelance days. A series of high-fidelity mobile concepts where I experimented with sophisticated effects and depth to create that scroll-stopping aesthetic. While the focus was on high-end execution in Figma and Photoshop, every pixel was still rooted in creating an engaging, intuitive flow that feels as good as it looks.

These designs were made before the AI era and were built entirely by hand using Figma, Illustrator and Photoshop.

The objectives of this personal project are...

Immersive Craft

Pushing Figma and Photoshop to their limits to create depth-heavy, tactile interfaces.

The "Dribbble" Standard

Mastering the high-end visual trends that grab attention in a crowded digital space.

Aesthetic Balance

Blending sophisticated visual effects with clean, functional UX for a high-impact mobile feel.
